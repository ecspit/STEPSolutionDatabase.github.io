STEP Solution Database

The STEP is the mathematics entrance exam used by the Univeristy of Caimbridge and some other univeristies as a part of their mathematics or maths-related courses' entry requirements. On this website you will find written solutions and discussions to various questions. Each document includes hints, a complete written solution and a discussion about the question

This website is by no means afiliated with the Univeristy of Caimbridge, OCR or the official STEP database.
